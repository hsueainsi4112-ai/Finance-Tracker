Our Story

Finance Tracker was created to support international students who come to the UK and face the challenge of managing their money for the first time. Many students struggle with high living expenses, unfamiliar banking systems, and cultural differences in budgeting.

Our goal is to provide a simple, smart, and easy-to-use platform that helps students track income, control daily expenses, and stay financially confident throughout their studies.

Over time, we aim to build a community where students can learn good money habits, share experiences, and support each other in managing life in the UK.

Who we are?

We are a student-focused platform built to help international students develop smart budgeting habits and financial confidence while living in the UK.

What we do?

We offer tools to track expenses, monitor spending patterns, set monthly budgets, and receive helpful insights through charts and reports.

Why choose us?

Our platform is designed specifically for international students — simple, visual, mobile-ready, and tailored to real student needs in the UK.

Our Features

We provide simple and powerful tools to help international students manage their finances, stay organized, and build strong budgeting habits while studying in the UK.

Expense Tracking

Record daily spending, categorize expenses, and keep track of your financial habits. All your expenses in one clean and simple dashboard.

Budget Planning

Set monthly budgets for food, transport, rent, and bills. Receive alerts if you exceed your planned budget to stay in control.

Reports & Insights

View charts, spending patterns, and detailed reports that help you understand your financial behavior and improve your budgeting.
